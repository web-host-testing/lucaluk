Executive Summary

Played a key role in revamping the corporate banking app for 130,000 customers by collaborating closely with business and IT teams.

Focused on aligning user needs with business objectives to deliver cost-effective and practical solutions.

Through strategic collaboration and UX research, we addressed critical pain points, enhanced user experience , and boosted customer engagement and satisfaction.
