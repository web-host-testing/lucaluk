Bank of China (Hong Kong)

Corporate Banking Revamp

Led research and UX design of web and mobile app revamp for 130k corporate customers

Problem

Low ratings in app store, had beeen receiving negative feedbacks from varioius channels

Problem

Customer services team frustrated with customers failing to complete simple tasks with the platform

Goals

  • Fix the journey that can bring most value
  • Introduce new features to benchmark industry competitors

Gather data

First studied the huge platform with complicated archetecture and products, mapped the structure and conduct expert review.

Next is to collect existing data and resources to better understanding what problems and opportunities we have.

I started getting in touch from numerous departs like customer service, marketing and data science team, to understand the behaviors and feedbacks from customers from three segments: Global corp, Mid corp and SME.

Cross Team Collaboration

After back and forth commmunications with them, I realized our relationship should be about mutual help, instead of merely asking materials from others.

User Interviews

We talked with customers from all 3 segments to get a clearer idea of their distinct needs.

Pain-points Analysis

Consolidating and analyzing all the research data, we have a full list of pain-points, including who is impacted and how painful they are with numbers and customer quotes.

The management can be more informed to decide which problem are more worth solving to the bank.

With the numbers like how much time customer service spent on reset password for customer, the business team can easily calculate the potential monetary value, which is crucial in the initiation of project funding.

Distinct Needs of Customer Segments

The platform is extremely complex especially to smaller companies, who do not need the comprehensive options and customizations.

Multiple-Company Owner

An opportunity that many SME owners have multiple companies, but fail to manage all bank accounts with single mobile phone.

Focus on SME

Based on the research, the management decided we can work on a simplified version tailored for the SME.

Metric

Increase mobile app downloads and monthly active user

Metric

Encourage the switch from physical token to mobile token.

Metric

    Reduce man hour related to digital pain-points of SME

Scope down in Feature Level

Now we know what problem to focus on, but in the end what feature to work on is still undertermined.

We need to make sure the cost of the solution can validate its potential return.

So we designed solutions that can solve the SME pain-points

Design Solution with IT & Business

Three teams work in the same room, the business tells what they want to achieve, we work the details on how it can work out, and the IT product managers explain to us know how the systems work and why some design may cost a lot more to build.

Accurate Cost Estimation

Togther we created the best possible and realistic customer journey flows for each potential feature for the IT department to provide a more accurate cost estimation.

Shortlisted Features

Out of all the features, here're the potential features for the first phase of revamp:

Reset Password

Allow user reset password online by verifying their identity with biometrics and ID card.

Mobile Dashboard

Optimize information archetechrue and add most wanted functionalities like monthly statement downloads.

Link Companies

Allow owners to use the same phone to login accounts of different companies.

Payment & Approval

Tailor the process for SME who do not require multiple level of approvals.

Test Concepts

One last step before kick-start of the project, we work with an agency to test if the design are the anwser for customer's pain-point. Other goals include testing usability of key flows and see if we missed anything.

We have 3 research focus:

  • Can users manage to navigate to the key functionlities after change in IA?
  • How do users compare existing and new payment flow?
  • Do users find new features like linking companies useful?

Hi-fi Prototyping

I designed a scenario combining all the key flows to test and document a test flow with remarks for the agency of what we want to learn in each step.

Focus Groups

The agency helped to test with 9 existing users, in which 4 of them have more than one company accounts.

Research Findings

The overall feedback reflects big improvements in user experience, with users believe can encourage usage.

We gain some insights on what information users want to see most and how to optimize copy and UI elements.

The biggest surprise is the heitation towards linking companies, the complexness of the development together brought a question mark to the project.

Huge improvement in home page

"This version improved so much, I can now easily get what I want like balance, monethly balance and payment."

Many nice touches in new payment process

"I didn't know how to set an unauthorized payee, this version is way easier!"

"Showing the payment fees upfront is a nice gesture"

"Allowing downloading the confirmation is great."

"This is a nice direction as it is troublesome managing the physical tokens."

"I can't see any reason for this, I don't want to risk getting a new physical at the branch, which will cause so much trouible! "

"I didn't know there is mobile token"

Should we proceed with the link company feature?

These seems to be the concerns of physical token users, we will need to understand more about how those using mobile tokens think. And if we want to replace them with mobile token eventually, we need to understand how some users are not switching.

Next Steps

I was not working with the company after the test, but if I had the chance, these are what I'll do:

Identify what info user need

Understand why customers want to see specific info before doing adjustments, avoid replacing any existing info that is valuable to some customers.

Optimize usability

Adust deisign and copy according to feedbacks and conduct usability test again.

Understand multiple companies customers

  • How mobile tokens customers think about the link companiies concept
  • Why physical token customers are not switching to mobile token

    • What I learned
