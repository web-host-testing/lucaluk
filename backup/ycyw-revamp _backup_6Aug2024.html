Understand Stakeholders Needs

The challenge is many stakeholders to consider, and the target audience of the sites are different for secondary schools, primary schools, kindergartens and college. Stakeholders include:

Client (Mothership + indiviual schools), IT vendors, internal team

So the first step was confirming the goal of everyone to avoid paying the price of change in direction in later stages.

Network structure Mapping

Help the internal team to understand the relationship between sub-brands of the network.

Analyze Existing Site

Understand current structure and content, highlighting how similar content placed in under different groups and named differently.

Align business goals with user goals

Identify target users corresponding with what we want them to do on the site and how to feel about the school.

Study target user: Future Parents

To achieve the main goal of the client: increase students enrollment, we need to understand the needs of key decision maker.

The user who can decide the buyin as well as doing the research will be parents who looking for school for their child.

So our focus is to understand the incentives and concerns behind applying for a YCYW school, as well as the common decision making factors of choosing a school.

Market Reports

Understand the demographics of future parents and students from research reports on international schools in China.

Social Listening

Study common sites and forums parents used to get info of international schools, to understand what they care and how they think of YCYW schools.

Root Cause Analysis

Combining what I learned, I logically make sense of their mental model with a mind map.

Content Strategy

With better understanding of future parents, we know what are the key problems to solve.

  • How to build trust?
  • How to attract parents effectively?
  • How to resolve key concerns?

How YCYW adcantages over others

From the social listening, we learned what aspects of YCYW actually attract future parents.

Competitor Analysis

Not only UI design and features, but how others solve the same problems we have.

Information Archetechture

The challenge is to make the same structure and page names across schools with distinct content, like kindergraden and university.

Evaluate Existing Structure

Discover key navigation issues, esp key info parents looking for with tree test.

Understand familiar structures

Learn where parents look for key info by studying stuctures of other international schools.

Explore naming of key pages

Use card sorting to confirm our assumptions in how parents group information.

Simplified Structure

From 40+ pages to 18 key pages, make use of tabs to make comprehensive info easy to reach without being overwhelming.

Wireframes with content strategy

Game plan of the whole site of what content and how to show them.

Client Communication

We had regular meetings to share our research and design updates and seek client's decision for the next actions.

Content Collaboration

Before proceeding with the copy writing, we put side notes in the wireframes showing what the content is about for client approval.

UI Design Explorations

We kept communicate with clients on the visual directions, key milestones include:

  1. Understand client's desirably look and feel
  2. Research on competitors on effective visual directions
  3. Showcase 3 options of home page for client
  4. Proceed with key pages design

Dev Communication

The first IT vendor we worked had been missing, not responsing to messages. In the end we end up changing vendor, which means going through the project with the dev once again.

Dev management

We just sent the files to the first vendor and hoping them to work on it. We learned to explain everything in detail to the dev in the beginning and set small milestones with the second vendor.

Design System

To facilitare developments and individual schools to customize the sites themselves, we make all the design as components with clear guidelines in a design system.

Features Highlights
