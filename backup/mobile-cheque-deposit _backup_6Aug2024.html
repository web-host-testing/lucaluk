Problem

Customers can only deposit cheques in branches, taking up lot of time and create a long queue.

Solution

Let customers to take picture of the cheque and deposit with bank mobile app

Goal

  • Reduce branch handling cost
  • increase mobile app usage

Complex Scenarios

Looking at the user requirement from the PM, we realized how complicated the project is.

Shocking number of combinations!

Customer Journey Mapping

In this situation, a detailed CJM showing all cases became crucial

20+ Unhappy Flows

While mapping the journey, we discovered many missed flows with the multi-layer limit and cheque checkings.

Questioning Logic with Dev

We checked the flow repeatedly with a dev in our team and fix issues from our blind spots

Error Scenarios Usability

When multiple errors happen at once, how can users understand what is happening and how to fix it?

What will happen if...

How users can fully understand why the cheque(s) can't get deposited and what they can do?

Usability Tests

2 rounds of tests are conducted with different error pages solutions

Shocking News from IT

After months sending all the designs with the PMs and IT happy with that, we received this "design" from IT:

Your design can't be done, this is what we can do

And what we see is something not only with UI not even close to the design, but a complete change to the multiple-error scenario user flow, with devasting usability.

Quick Workaround

We called an emergency meeting, and instead of pointing fingers to eachother, we seiae the chance to communicate with IT to understand why the design can't be done, so we can throw ideas on actionable workarounds with user experience passing the baseline.

UI Adjustment Guide

Created a detailed guide pointing out areas to adjust, with priotization for devs to work for best results before deadline.
